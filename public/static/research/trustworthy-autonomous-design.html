Integrating Human Logic & Deep Learning for Trustworthy Autonomous Design

Funding Agency:

DoD

Amount:

$750K

Duration:

2021-2024

Project Overview

What are the correct ways to interface symbolic knowledge and deep learning models in cyber-physical systems that allow humans to understand and intervene decision-making processes?

Research Questions

  • How can we maintain human interpretability in AI-driven autonomous systems?
  • What are the optimal ways to combine symbolic reasoning with deep learning?
  • How can we ensure human operators can effectively intervene when necessary?

Approach

Our approach focuses on developing hybrid AI systems that leverage both the power of deep learning and the transparency of symbolic reasoning. We're creating frameworks that allow human experts to understand, validate, and when necessary, override autonomous system decisions in safety-critical scenarios.
